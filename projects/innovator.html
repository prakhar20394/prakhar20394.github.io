AR Playground

AR Playground is an innovative AR app designed to provide an immersive and experiential learning experience for children. It combines intentional play and age-appropriate (Age 4-6) challenges to facilitate cognitive development. The app utilizes augmented reality to transform empty spaces into engaging play areas, where children can learn phonetics, alphabets, sorting, stacking, and even create their own augmented reality worlds. AR Playground was developed with a focus on promoting sensory experiences, movement, and interactive learning.

Sensory-Pathway

We designed a controlled environment with specially designed paths to activate different senses and body movements. We designed and set it up for Jamghat, an NGO for Street Kids in Delhi, it was later adopted as a social entrepreneurship initiative by Enactus owing to its effectiveness. By engaging in sensory movements, it can help develop sensorimotor skills, interact with the environment, and support their overall well-being.

Happy Meal Project

Happy Meal is an educational app that promotes healthy eating habits among children aged 5-10. Through interactive games, children learn about nutrition, cooking, and making healthy food choices. The app addresses concerns regarding fussy eating habits and childhood obesity, empowering children to make informed decisions about their diet. It enables parents and educators to teach children about healthy eating habits in a fun and interactive way. With features like a cooking game and a nutrient checker, the app encourages children to engage with food and assess its nutritional value. Happy Meal creates a positive relationship with food while promoting a healthy lifestyle.
