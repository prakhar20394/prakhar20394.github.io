AR Playground

Augmented Reality Game Design

AR Playground is an immersive Augmented Reality app that offers experiential learning for children. Designed for ages 4-6, it blends intentional play and age-appropriate challenges to foster cognitive development. Through augmented reality, empty spaces are transformed into engaging play areas where kids can learn phonetics, alphabets, sorting, stacking, and even create their own AR worlds. The app prioritizes sensory experiences, movement, and interactive learning, providing a unique educational experience.

Sensory-Pathway

Space Design Social Work

We designed a controlled environment with custom-designed paths to stimulate various senses and body movements. This setup was implemented at Jamghat, an NGO for Street Kids in Delhi. Recognizing its effectiveness, the initiative was later adopted as a social entrepreneurship project by Enactus. Through sensory movements, this environment promotes the development of sensorimotor skills, encourages interaction with the surroundings, and enhances overall well-being.

Happy Meal Project

Augmented Reality Game Design Deployed Product

Happy Meal is an educational app that fosters healthy eating habits in children aged 5-10. Through interactive games, kids learn about nutrition, cooking, and making wise food choices. The app tackles fussy eating and childhood obesity concerns, empowering children to make informed dietary decisions. It offers a fun and interactive way for parents and educators to teach kids about healthy eating. With features like a cooking game and nutrient checker, it encourages engagement with food and promotes a positive relationship with a healthy lifestyle.
