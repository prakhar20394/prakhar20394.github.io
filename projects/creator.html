Resume

I'm a Creator

I'm someone who absolutely loves creating things. I get a real thrill out of coming up with new ideas, putting in the work to bring them to life, and watching them flourish and succeed. It's incredibly satisfying to see something I've imagined become a reality. I'm always on the lookout for new opportunities to create something new and exciting.

1Pixel Design Conf'23

In January 2023, my team and I had an amazing opportunity to organize the 1Pixel Design Conf'23, India's biggest student-led design conference. We brought together over 15 speakers from top organizations like Microsoft, Samsung, Oppo, Wadhwani AI, and DigiToons. It was a blast with 200+ participants from 25+ design institutes joining us. It was an incredible experience that taught us the power of teamwork, overcoming challenges, and making connections. We'll always cherish the memories and lessons from this unforgettable journey.

Business Blasters - Coach

In 2022, I had the privilege of being involved with Business Blasters, a startup competition that aimed to empower government school students in building their own startups with seed funding from the Delhi Government. I had the opportunity to mentor a team, guiding them in branding and marketing strategies, and helping them develop their brand story and final pitch video. The team, known as 'She.Chirps', showcased incredible dedication and talent, eventually making it to the top 100 out of a staggering 20,000+ participating teams in the inaugural Business Blasters competition. It was a rewarding experience to witness their growth and success throughout the competition.

Volunteers.Covihelp

During the second wave of Covid in 2021, my friends and I co-founded Volunteers.Covihelp, a volunteer-based service providing medical supplies and checking resource availability. We assisted over 1700+ individuals and led a team of 300+ volunteers from around the world. Our efforts were featured in leading news outlets like Hindustan Times, New Indian Express, Republic, and Firstpost. We also raised over 1.31 Lac for a 12-year-old girl's cancer treatment. Additionally, I led the Social Media and Design team to raise awareness about our cause.
