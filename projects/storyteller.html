I'm a Storyteller

From videos to conversational agents, physical toys to games, I immerse myself in various art forms to create meaningful experiences. Beyond the Books

UX/UI Design

Beyond the Books was a project that harnessed the power of storytelling to educate middle school students on essential real-world topics, including Money, Banking, and subjects aligned with the New Education Policy (NEP). We crafted a presentation pitch video for the product, drawing inspiration from Apple's advertisements and teasers. Our aim was to create a professional video that generated excitement and anticipation, effectively showcasing the product's value and potential.

Omnisense - The Presentation Game

Game Design Narrative

Omnisense offers a distinctive and captivating storytelling style that sets it apart. Departing from traditional linear narratives, our goal was to provide a non-linear storytelling experience, granting users greater control over the presentation. By allowing users to navigate at their own pace, collect food items akin to a Swiggy rider, and explore different pathways to access presentation screens, this approach encourages a deeper understanding and connection with the content. The personalized and impactful learning journey provided by Omnisense ensures a meaningful and engaging experience for users.
