I'm a Storyteller

As a passionate storyteller, I am driven to explore a wide range of mediums, both linear and non-linear, in order to craft compelling narratives. From videos to conversational agents, physical toys to games, I immerse myself in various art forms to create meaningful experiences. My ultimate goal is to foster empathy and forge connections through the powerful art of storytelling. Continuously pushing boundaries, I actively seek out new techniques to effectively communicate ideas and provoke thought. With a spirit of experimentation, I am dedicated to honing my craft and pushing the boundaries of storytelling to captivate and engage audiences in unique and impactful ways.

UX/UI Design Research

We used a special storytelling style - slow-paced hand-drawn whiteboard sketches. It helps create a strong emotional connection with the audience. Beyond the Books

UX/UI Design

Beyond the Books was a project aimed at leveraging the power of storytelling to educate middle school students about crucial real-world topics, such as Money, Banking, and other subjects introduced in the new education policy (NEP). We crafted a presentation pitch video for the product, highlighting its key features and core focus. Inspired by the style of Apple's advertisments and teasers, our goal was to create a highly professional video that generates excitement and buzz, effectively showcasing the product's value and potential.

Omnisense - The Presentation Game

Game Design Narrative

Omnisense presents a unique and engaging storytelling style that captivates users. Breaking away from traditional linear narratives, we wanted to offer a non-linear storytelling experience that gives users more control over the presentation. By allowing users to navigate the content at their own pace, collect food items (just like a swiggy rider) and explore different pathways to see the presentation screens, this approach enabled users to dive deep into the ideas and concepts being presented, fostering a deeper understanding and connection and ensured that users have a personalized and impactful learning journey.n.
