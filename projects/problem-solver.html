Google Drive Me Crazy

UX Design Research Deployed Product

Led the "Google Drive Me Crazy" project, which aimed to redesign the user experience of Google Drive. By conducting in-depth user research, we identified and addressed issues related to navigation, small UI elements, lack of space analyzer, and visualizer in the interface.
Our efforts resulted in a significant 102% increase in satisfaction levels based on the System Usability Scale (SUS). We also achieved a notable 35.5% reduction in time spent on basic Google Drive tasks.
