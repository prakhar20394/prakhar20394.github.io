Google Drive Me Crazy

UX Design Research Deployed Product

I led the "Google Drive Me Crazy" project, focused on enhancing the user experience of Google Drive. Through comprehensive user research, we identified and addressed navigation issues, tiny-UI elements, and the lack of space analyzer and visualizer in the interface. Our efforts yielded remarkable results, including a notable 102% increase in satisfaction levels measured by the System Usability Scale (SUS). Additionally, we achieved a significant 35.5% reduction in the time spent on basic Google Drive tasks, enhancing overall efficiency and user productivity.
