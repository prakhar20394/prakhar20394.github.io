PAIZ Project

Robotics Research Workshop Design

I contributed in the PAIZ Project, a collaborative initiative between the University of Oulu in Finland and the Living Lab at IIIT-Delhi. Our project centered around studying child-robot interactions in unsupervised settings, utilizing the Nao V6 Robot. With a specific focus on the Indian context, our objective was to delve into the intricacies of these interactions and examine their influence on children's experiences.

SafAR

UX/UI Design Research Augmented Reality History Unity

I am presently involved in the SafAR project, an Augmented Reality-based platform that aims to provide meaningful experiences for tourists and learners visiting historical sites. The project specifically focuses on developing a module dedicated to the renowned Qutub Minar in Delhi. Currently, we are in the research phase, exploring existing literature and the latest advancements in the field of AR and tourism to inform our project's development.

Research at Wadhwani AI

Focus Group Discussion Field Visits

As part of the General-Ophthalmology solution for an image-based diagnostics system, we conducted visits to AIIMS Delhi and Trilokpuri vision center to gain insights into their existing resources and activities. Through various research methods such as Focus Group Discussions, Shadowing, formal and informal interactions, we gathered valuable information. Subsequently, we compiled a comprehensive research guide, establishing a solid foundation for the entire solution space.
