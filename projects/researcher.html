I'm a Researcher

I am always curious to discover more and learn all the way. I am driven by deep desire to explore and know something knew. I wish to uncover new insights. I believe research is the most important stepping stone in any decision, especially when it comes to design. A good research sets the foundation for usable products, and can dictate the future of the product

PAIZ Project

Robotics Research Workshop Design

I contributed to the PAIZ Project, a collaboration between the University of Oulu in Finland and the Living Lab at IIIT-Delhi. Our project explored child-robot interactions in unsupervised settings using the Nao V6 Robot. Specifically focusing on the Indian context, we aimed to understand the complexities of these interactions and their impact on children's experiences.

SafAR

UX/UI Design Research Augmented Reality History

Currently working on the SafAR project, a Augmented Reality based platform focussing on creating meaningful experiences for tourists and learners at historical sites. This project would focus on creating a module focussing on the Qutub Minar in Delhi. Currently we are in the research phase focussing on the existing literature and the current state of the art in the field of AR and tourism.

Research at Wadhwani AI

Focus Group Discussion Field Visits

Working in the General-Ophthalmology solution for Image based diagnostics system, we visited AIIMS Delhi & Trilokpuri vision center, focussing on the current resources and activities. We conducted Focus Group Discussions, Shadowing, formal & informal discussions and later, prepared a research guide, setting a foundation for the entire solution space.
