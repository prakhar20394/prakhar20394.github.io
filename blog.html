My Experience working at WadhwaniAI

May-July2023
UX / UI Design

In the summer of 2023, I had the incredible opportunity to intern at Wadhwani AI, a socially-focused organization driven by Artificial Intelligence. Their mission revolved around utilizing AI for the betterment of society. During my internship, I had the privilege of working in the E-Health domain, collaborating with prestigious institutions like the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, AIIMS Delhi, and Rishikesh. Contributing to such meaningful work, merging the power of AI with healthcare, was an incredibly rewarding experience.

Nao V6 - A new learning opportunity of Robotics and Research

June 2022 - January 2023
Research Project

I was part of the PAIZ Project, a collaborative project between the University of Oulu in Finland and the Living Lab at IIIT-Delhi. In this project, we explored child-robot interactions in unsupervised settings, using the Nao V6 Robot as a tool. With a focus on the Indian context, we delve into the complexities of these interactions and their profound impact on children's experiences and perceptions. Now, we are writing a research paper that aims to shed light on the intricate dynamics of child-robot interactions. Through this, we hope to contribute to the ever-growing knowledge in this field and pave the way for advancements that enhance human-robot interactions in educational and social settings.

Designing for Scale - My experience working at Government eMarketplace

May-July 2022
UX/UI Design, Design System

I had the privilege of interning as a UX/UI Design Intern at Government e Marketplace (GeM). Working with the Design team of MSPs - Infibeam Avenues and Intellect Design, my focus was on revamping the User Interface of the GeM portal, enabling transparent and efficient procurement for government departments. During my internship, We successfully addressed 200+ user-issues, enhancing the Registration & Bid participation module and reducing user fall-off rate. Additionally, we developed design guidelines for the platform, ensuring consistency and standardization. I gained valuable learning experience and exposure in the world of UX/UI Design.
